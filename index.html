Overview

I am advised by Dr. Daniel Gibney; I study graph theory and algorithms in the contexts of strings, quantum computing, and data streams under him at the University of Texas at Dallas. Tangentially, I am also interested in computer vision and multimodal computing, logic programming, number theory, and cryptography.
