Advisors

Dr. Gibney is Mr. Doan's current research advisor.
Dr. Gibney's research interests lie at the intersection of bioinformatics, computational biology, algorithm design, computational complexity, and quantum algorithms. His work explores innovative approaches within these domains, with a focus on advancing theoretical frameworks and practical applications.

Dr. Fox is also Mr. Doan's current research advisor.
Dr. Fox's research explores the intersection of topology and graph theory, investigating how topological structures can facilitate efficient computations on graphs and what graph properties enable such speedups. Dr. Fox has concentrated on the algorithmic foundations of computer science, emphasizing their connections to geometry and topology.

Dr. Satpute is Mr. Doan's pedagogical advisor.
Dr. Satpute's research was in applying combinatorial optimization algorithms for solving natural language-processing problems and wireless communication problems.

Dr. Tian was Mr. Doan's research advisor for the Fall of 2023, and Spring and Summer of 2024.
Dr. Yapeng Tian leads the Computer Vision and Multimodal Computing (CVMC) Lab. His research tackles fundamental problems in computer vision, computer audition, and machine learning, with applications in multisensory perception, computational photography, AR/VR, accessibility, and healthcare.
